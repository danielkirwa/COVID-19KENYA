Corona virus travel guide and how to travel safely IN KENYA


Basic travel guidline...
During this period of COVID-19 you are adviced to travel only when it is necessry and if posible stay at home all time.
Avoid croweded public means of transport since you will be at high risk of contracting the disease
Always wear mask while travelimg and avoid touching surfaces or objects

Travel Restriction...
Currently there are travel restriction in kenya put in place in order to prevent further spread of the COVID-19 in the county:

  1. Currently international flights remain grounded
  2. No traveling in and out of NAIROBI and MOMBASA cities
  3. Restricted Border crossing

Public trasport guidelines...

  1. Public service vehicle should not carry more than 60% capacity
  2. You should sanitize your hand before boarding a vehicle
  3. You should wear while boarding and also while in the vehicle

Place and counties should try visit less frequently...
