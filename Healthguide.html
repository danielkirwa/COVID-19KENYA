Corona virus health guide and how to stay save


Coronaviruses is a virus that causes illness to human.The most recent discovered coronavirus is COCID-19.

What is COVOD-19 ?
This is an infectious disease caused by coronavirus which causes respiratory infection.The disease outbreak began in Wuhan China in December 2019 and is now a global pandemic.

Symptoms of COVID-19 ?
Common symptoms are :

  1. Fever
  2. Dry cough
  3. Difficulty in breathing
  4. Tiredness
  5. Sore throat
  6. Headache

How does COCID-19 spread ?
It can be trasmited from one person to another through droplets form nose or mouth of an infected person when sneezing coughing or even speaking.This droplets can land on objects or surfaces hence becoming trasmission media since when we touch this contaminated objects or surfaces and then touching our eyes,nose or mouth without washing our hands we may contract the virus.

How can you avoid COVID-19 ?
There are several measures to protect yourself from contracting and also trasmmiting the disease to others.This can be achived by practicing general hygiene:

  1. Wash your hands regulary with saop and runing water or sanitizer
  2. Wear mask while in public places
  3. Maintain 1 meter distance from other persons
  4. Avoid touching surface and objects in public place
  5. Avoid touching your face
  6. Avoid social meetings
  7. While coughing use disposable tissue or cover your mouth with your elbo
  8. Avoid unnecessary travel

How to use/wear mask ?

  1. Before touching the mask clean your hand first
  2. Take the mask and inpect for tear or hole
  3. Ensure the proper side ofthe mask faces outwords
  4. Pull the elastic loops behind your ears to hold the mask in place
  5. Place the mask on the face so to moulds to the shape of your nose
  6. Pull the mask down to cover your mouth and chin
  7. Do not touch the mask while in use
  8. After use pull of the elastic loops off your ears
  9. Discard the used mask in a close bin immediately
  10. Wash your hands with water and soap or alcohol-based
